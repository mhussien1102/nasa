Solution

Imagine our planet is a big house. We live here with all the animals and plants. But there's a problem: the house is getting too hot! Why is it getting hot? It's because we're using too much energy from things like coal and oil. When we burn these, they release a gas called carbon dioxide (CO2). This gas is like a blanket around the Earth, making it warmer. How can we make the house cooler? Switch to green energy: Instead of using coal and oil, we can use energy from the sun, wind, and water. This is like turning on a fan to cool down the house. Save energy: We can turn off lights when we leave a room and take shorter showers. This is like closing the windows to keep the heat out. Plant trees: Trees help to clean the air by sucking up CO2. Planting more trees is like opening a window to let fresh air in. Recycle and reduce waste: When we throw away trash, it can release a gas called methane, which is also bad for the planet. Recycling and reducing waste is like cleaning up the mess in our house. Can you help? You can help by doing small things like turning off lights and using less water. You can also plant trees and recycle. Together, we can make our planet a cooler and healthier place to live!
